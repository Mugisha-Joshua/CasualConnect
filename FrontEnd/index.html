Why CasualConnect?

In many African communities, especially Rwanda, casual jobs go unnoticed. We make it easy for anyone to post or find informal work — from domestic help to shop assistants. No CVs, no long forms. Just real jobs for real people.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to bridge the gap between job seekers and local opportunities by providing a platform that is open, accessible, and tailored for informal workers. Whether you are looking for a quick gig or need help for your home or shop, CasualConnect is here to make the process smooth and transparent.

  • Accessible: Works on any device, even with slow internet.
  • Barrier-free: No registration or CV needed to browse jobs.
  • Empowering: Supports youth, women, and small businesses in local communities.

How Does It Work?

Post a Job

Employers or households post their job in seconds. Just add title, location, description, and contact—no technical skills required!

Browse & Filter

Job seekers browse available jobs by location or category. Designed to be mobile-first and easy to use for everyone.

Apply & Connect

Contact the employer or apply directly—no need for a CV. Get hired and start working fast!
