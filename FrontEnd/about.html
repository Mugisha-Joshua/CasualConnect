About CasualConnect

Bridging the gap between local job seekers and small businesses or households in Rwanda looking for informal workers.

Our Mission

To connect youth and informal workers in Rwanda with real, short-term job opportunities—without the barriers of CVs or complicated apps. We empower both job seekers and local employers, creating a simple, web-based platform accessible on any device.

The Problem

Many young people and informal workers in Rwanda struggle to find work. Most jobs are never advertised online, and current job platforms are designed for office jobs—not household help or casual labor. Instead, people rely on word-of-mouth or go door-to-door, making it difficult for both job seekers and employers to connect efficiently.

Our Solution

CasualConnect is a lightweight, mobile-friendly job board for informal and entry-level work. No account needed to browse. No CV required. We make it easy to post, find, and apply for jobs in a few taps—helping communities thrive and increasing financial stability for all.

Our Values

  • Accessibility: Designed for everyone, even on low-end devices.
  • Trust & Simplicity: No complicated forms or apps. Real jobs, real people, real impact.
  • Community Impact: Reducing unemployment and boosting Rwanda’s economy by connecting those who need work with those who need help.

Join Us in Making a Difference

Whether you are seeking work or need a helping hand, CasualConnect is your bridge to opportunity in your community.
